# Domain Name Brief for ChatGPT

## What is this platform?

Anonymous peer-to-peer support platform for people dealing with addiction. It connects people who need help (clients) with peers who have lived experience with addiction and recovery.

**Core values:**
- Zero accounts, zero identity, zero personal data
- End-to-end encrypted chat — server cannot read messages
- Wallet-based verification (Bitcoin/Litecoin balance check)
- Works over Tor

**Target users:**
- People in active addiction who need to talk to someone who's been there
- People in recovery who want to give back by supporting others
- Operates globally: currently targeting English, Russian, Spanish, Georgian markets
- Cities: Tbilisi, Moscow, Madrid, New York, London, Amsterdam, Berlin, Nha Trang, Da Nang, Kyiv, Riga

## Current name

**NA Room** — inspired by NA (Narcotics Anonymous) "rooms" where people share stories. The metaphor: an anonymous meeting room, open 24/7, anywhere in the world.

The existing placeholder domain is `naroom.net` but we're open to alternatives.

## What we need

Suggest **10–15 domain name options** that are:

1. **Short** — ideally 6–10 characters, easy to type in Tor Browser
2. **Memorable** — someone in crisis can recall it
3. **Neutral or hopeful** — not clinical, not dark, not preachy
4. **Globally understood** — works across languages and cultures
5. **Available to register** — avoid obvious trademark conflicts

Consider:
- `.net`, `.org`, `.com`, `.io`, `.to`, `.app`, `.care`, `.help`, `.support`, `.chat`, `.space`, `.link`
- Onion-first mindset: the domain is secondary to .onion, but still matters for discoverability
- The name should NOT sound like a medical service, hotline, or government resource
- It SHOULD feel like a quiet, private room — peer-to-peer, human, low-pressure

## Constraints

- Avoid: "anonymous", "crypto", "bitcoin", "wallet", "drug", "narco", "addict" in the domain itself — too literal or stigmatizing
- Avoid: anything that sounds like a crisis line or emergency service
- Avoid: overly startup-ish names (no "-ly", "-ify", "-hub")
- The brand name and domain can be different (e.g. brand = "NA Room", domain = `quietroom.net`)

## Examples of the tone we're going for

Think: quiet, human, accessible. Like walking into a room where people understand without judgment.

Metaphors that resonate with the concept:
- Room / space / place
- Circle / group
- Bridge / path
- Light / dawn / morning
- Voice / speak / talk

## Output format

For each suggestion provide:
1. Domain name (with TLD)
2. Why it fits
3. Potential issues (trademark, stigma, confusion with other services)
4. Alternative TLDs for the same name

Then rank your top 5 by: memorability + availability likelihood + emotional fit.
